Professional

I graduated from Western Washington University in 2017 without any concrete plans for a career. After graduation I spent time figuring out what I wanted to do, which led me to move down to Portland with my girlfriend to start a career in Sales. After 2 1/2 years I learned Sales was not my passion, so I started to explore other avenues. This led me to enroll at Epicodus. I had been interested in coding for a few years and after exploring some free resources online I decided to take the plunge and began to pursue a career in tech development.
